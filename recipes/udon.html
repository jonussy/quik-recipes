Warm and Filling Udon

The best pick me up after a cold, rainy day!

This dish is a small, quick and easy meal to keep your belly full and warm

Ingredients

  • Udon Noodle
  • Dashi Broth
  • Choice of Toppings

Recipe instructions

  1. Make Dashi (Use water and dashi packet, bring to boil)
  2. Add soy sauce, mirin, sugar, and salt to Dashi broth
  3. Cook udon noodles (set aside in bowl)
  4. Combine Dashi Broth and Noodles in serving bowl
  5. Add choice of toppings (Green Onion, Chashu, Enoki Mushrooms)
