Eggy Egg Salad Sandwich

A quick and easy egg sandwich that explodes with flavor and creaminess

This is a quick and eggy suprise you owe to your tastebuds!

Ingredients

  • Six Large Eggs
  • Bread of Choice
  • Mayonnaise
  • Mustard
  • Green Onions
  • Salt, Pepper, & Paprika

Recipe instructions

  1. Place six large eggs in pot with cold water and let reach to rolling boil
  2. When water reaches rolling boil, cover pot and place on low heat for 7 minutes
  3. Drain water and add eggs to bowl of ice water
  4. Peel eggs and chop eggs to ones desired chop thickness
  5. Combine 2 tablespoons of mayonnaise, 1 tablespoon of mustard, pinch of pepper, salt, & paprika
  6. Serve on bread or with choice of crackers
