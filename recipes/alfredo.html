Thick & Creamy Alfredo

The perfect dish for your spicy senorita!

This is a quick and painless dinner idea for your date night!

Ingredients

  • Alfredo Noodles
  • Heavy Whipping Cream
  • Butter
  • Minced Garlic
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Salt & Pepper

Recipe instructions

  1. Warm up your butter and cream in a large sauce pan over low heat
  2. After simmering for 2 minutes, whisk in garlic and seasonings
  3. Whisk constantly or one minute to allow garlic to cook
  4. Add your parmesan cheese and stir until cheese has melted and sauce is smooth
  5. Boil Alfredo noodles to your liking and add to large sauce pan with a tablespoon of pasta water to help thicken up your sauce
